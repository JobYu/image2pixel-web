Image2pixel Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2025

Welcome to the Image2pixel application ("App"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information Collection

The types of information we collect include:

  • Information You Provide: When using the App, we only access your device's photo library with your explicit permission to process the images you choose. These images are processed on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Automatically Collected Information: We collect basic data about your use of the App, such as app crash reports and performance data, to help us improve the App experience.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, transaction information is processed by Apple, and we do not collect or store your payment details.

2. Information Use

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Process the image conversion features you request
  • Respond to your questions or requests
  • Develop new features and fix issues

3. Information Storage

Image2pixel is a privacy-centered application:

  • Your images and settings are saved locally on your device and are not uploaded to the cloud
  • We use Apple's standard security tools (such as KeyChain) to securely store purchase records
  • We do not maintain any user database; all processing is completed on your device

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. However, information may be shared in the following circumstances:

  • When we use third-party service providers to support our App's functionality
  • To comply with legal requirements, enforce our policies, or protect our or others' rights and safety

5. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: Processes in-app purchases and subscription management
  • Apple Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data to improve App performance

These services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend that you review these policies to understand how they handle your data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on the laws in your country/region, you may have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Restriction or objection to the processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the contact information provided below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove this information from our systems.

8. Privacy Policy Updates

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. After updates, we will post the new Privacy Policy on this page and notify you of significant changes in the App. We recommend that you regularly review this page to stay informed about the latest information.
